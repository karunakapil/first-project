ROUND 1

Maathi yosi is a fun game where the participant has to provide completely unrelated answer to each question posed by the host

ROUND 2

There are many times when one needs to be witty, persuasive,cunning and evasive!! Well we provide you a stage to display your expertise in such diverse conditions and emerge as the bluff master.

ROUND 3

In this event, a participant should speak for a minute on the topic, trying not to break a dozen rules while he/she has 5 other players waiting to pounce on half a mistake, so that they can speak.

round 1

  • Should not give the same answer.
  • Should not say the same word thrice.
  • Should not ask questions to the host.
  • Should not take more than 3 seconds to answer.
  • Should sustain for complete 2 minutes to get to the next round.
  • Can answer either in English or Tamil.

    • round 2

    • Only individual participants are allowed.
    • Answers provided must be to the point..
    • The participants are not allowed to talk to each other during the event..
    • The participants must not take more than 10 seconds to answer the question..
    • The participants are not allowed to question the host..
    • The participant must not repeat the same word / answer twice.
      • round 3
      • The Jammers are challenged to speak for one minute on a given subject without repetition, hesitation or deviation.
      • Hesitation is watched very strictly.
      • Deviation means deviating from the subject (ie) deviating from the truth.
      • A Jammer scores a point for making a correct challenge against whomever is speaking, while the speaker gets a point if the challenge is deemed incorrect.
      • A Speaker also scores a point if they are the person speaking when the 60 seconds expires.
